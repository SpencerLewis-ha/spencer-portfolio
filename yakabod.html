Yakabod

1 Challenge

Enterprise software serving government and security teams requires an exceptionally high reliability bar — yet inconsistent test coverage and documentation gaps were making it difficult to catch defects before release.

2 Solution

Over two summers I worked systematically through regression, acceptance, integration, and feature validation workflows — logging defects with detailed notes, contributing to automation pipelines, and helping improve documentation standards the team could build on.

3 Impact

The structured approach I helped establish gave the engineering team greater confidence at release — and the experience gave me a deep understanding of how quality assurance fits into the full software development lifecycle.
